Carbondale, PA - USA

Location

Carbondale, PA is located about 30 minutes North East of Scranton, PA.

About

Weather

The weather in Carbondale is about what you would expect of Pennsylvania weather. The summers are nice and warm, perfect for a swim, and the winters are cold and snowy.

Population

Carbondale has a population of around 8,812 people.

Sports

Highschool sports is popular in this small town with football, basketball, and baseball being the top sports played in this area.
